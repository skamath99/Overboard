Overboard!

A two-player abstract strategy game of shoves and sacrifices. Move up to two pieces, then push — force any one of your opponent's pieces off the board and you win. Play with friends over Game Center, battle strangers in the ranked lobby, or pass one phone around the table.

Note: All the code here was created by Claude Code. None of it was reviewed by a human.

Features

  • Pass & Play — full game on a single device
  • Online matches with friends via Game Center invites
  • Ranked lobby with Elo-based matchmaking
  • Match history with move-by-move replay
  • How-to-play guide built in

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2026

Overboard! is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

Overboard! does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with the developer. The app itself has no servers and no accounts.

Game Center

Online multiplayer, matchmaking, and the rating leaderboard are provided by Apple's Game Center. When you play online, your Game Center display name, your moves, and your in-app rating are shared with your opponent and processed by Apple in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. You can play locally (Pass & Play) without signing in to Game Center at all.

Data Storage

Match history and your rating are stored locally on your device. Online match state is stored by Game Center for the duration of a match. No information is transmitted to the developer or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Overboard! does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Control

You can delete match history from within the app, and leave or cancel online matches at any time. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data from your device.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected here with a new "Last Updated" date.
